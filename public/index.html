How to play

You control ONE player. Score more goals than the other team before the clock hits zero. 3 minutes per match.

Desktop
Move: WASD or arrow keys · Sprint: hold SHIFT
Pass: SPACE or J · Shoot: hold K for power · Lob: L

Phone
Move: drag on the left half · buttons on the right: PASS, SHOOT (hold = power), LOB, SPRINT

Ball control
Run onto the ball to take it — it sticks to your feet while you dribble; sprinting knocks it further ahead. PASS auto-aims your best teammate. SHOOT locks onto goal — placement follows your facing. LOB floats a through ball to your most advanced teammate. Defending, PASS becomes a tackle lunge — win it clean or stumble.

Camera
Press C (or the 🎥 button) to cycle: Broadcast → Third person → First person → Overhead. More options in ⚙️ Settings.
