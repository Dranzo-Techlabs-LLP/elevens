How to play

You control ONE player. Score more goals than the other team before the clock hits zero. 3 minutes per match.

Desktop
Move: WASD or arrow keys
Kick: hold SPACE to charge, release to kick — quick tap = pass, full charge = shot

Phone
Move: touch and drag anywhere on the left half of the screen
Kick: hold the KICK button to charge, release to kick

Tips
Run into the ball to dribble. Kicks fly in the direction you're facing. Empty slots are filled by bots — a friend can join mid-match and take one over.
