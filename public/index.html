How to play

You control ONE player. Score more goals than the other team before the clock hits zero. 3 minutes per match.

Desktop
Move: WASD or arrow keys
Kick: hold SPACE, release to strike

Phone
Move: touch and drag anywhere on the left half of the screen
Kick: hold the KICK button, release to strike

Ball control
Run onto the ball to take it — it sticks to your feet while you dribble. Charge decides the ball: tap = short pass · half hold = driven long pass · full hold = lofted ball / shot. Kicks go where you're facing. Stand in the path to block or trap it.

Camera
Press C (or the 🎥 button) to cycle: Broadcast → Third person → First person → Overhead. More options in ⚙️ Settings.
